User Personas


Task Analysis

High-Level Tasks:

1. Explore Destinations

Goal: Discover blogs and information about places around the world.

Subtasks:

  • Zoom and pan around globe to view destinations.
  • Click on map markers to open blogs.
  • Filter blogs by destination, tags, author, etc.
  • Save blogs to read later.

2. Create Travel Blog

Goal: Share personal travel stories and experiences.

Subtasks:

  • Select destination on map.
  • Enter blog title and content.
  • Upload photos/videos.
  • Add tags relevant to the blog.
  • Preview and publish the blog.
  • Share the published blog link.

3. Interact with Community

Goal: Connect with other travelers and blog authors.

Subtasks:

  • Comment on blogs.
  • Follow other users.
  • Join interest-based travel groups.
  • Direct message other users.
  • Get notifications of new blogs from followed users.

4. Discover Events

Goal: Stay updated on local festivals and events.

Subtasks:

  • Filter map view by events layer.
  • Click event pin to see details.
  • RSVP or express interest in attending.
  • Share event details with others.
