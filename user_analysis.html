Conclusion

User class 1: Content Creator/Traveler

  • Budget Constraints: Our Content Creator/Traveler is passionate about exploring new places but is often constrained by a limited budget. Finding cost-effective ways to have exciting travel experiences can be quite frustrating. While he loves to capture and share his adventures, staying within budget is a constant concern.
  • Loneliness: Traveling solo can sometimes lead to feelings of isolation and solitude. He wishes he had someone to share his thrilling adventures with, not just for companionship but also to enhance the experiences he documents.
  • Capturing Unique Experiences: Another challenge for our Content Creator/Traveler is capturing unique and captivating content. He strives to stand out in the crowded world of travel content creation and needs new ideas to keep his audience engaged.

User class 2: Student Traveler

  • Limited Funds: As a student, financial constraints are a significant frustration. Traveling often seems like an expensive endeavor, and he's always on the lookout for budget-friendly ways to explore new places. He wishes for guidance on average budgets for different destinations and suggestions for affordable trips.
  • Reality vs. Social Media: The Student Traveler often finds himself comparing his own experiences to what he sees on social media platforms like Instagram. This comparison can lead to disappointment as he realizes that his real-life experiences may not always match up to the curated and idealized versions he encounters online.
  • Time Management: Balancing travel adventures with academic commitments can be challenging. He needs tips on time management to make the most of his trips without compromising his studies.
